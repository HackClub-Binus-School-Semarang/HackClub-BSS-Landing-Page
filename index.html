FAQ – About Hack Club @ Binus School Semarang

  • What is Hack Club @ Binus School Semarang?
    HackClub @ Binus School Semarang is a student-led makers and coding club where students come together to explore technology, learn to code, build hardware and software projects, and collaborate on creative ideas.
  • Do I need to know how to code to join?
    Not at all! Hack Club @ Binus School Semarang welcomes everyone who is commited to learn and dedicate their time.
  • What do you do in meetings?
    Workshops, demos, and project time.
  • When and where do you meet?
    Schedule will be announced soon. Stay Tuned!
  • Who can join?
    Any student from Binus School Semarang is welcome.
  • Is there a membership fee?
    No, joining Hack Club is completely free.
  • How can I get free access to Brilliant Premium?
    You can get free access to Brilliant Premium by becoming a Hack Club member. Contact the club organizers to verify your membership.
  • How can I join?
    Use the form. If you have questions, contact us via email.
